# Summary

Design based here : 
http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:533029
(for E3D_v5_v6_40mm_Duct.stl and E3D_v5_v6_40mm_Duct_Mirrored.stl)
and here : 
http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:958827


---------------------------------------------------------

If you want the SINGLE fan version print : 
E3Dv5-Holder.stl OR E3Dv6-Holder.stl (pick your extruder version)
AND
E3D_v5_v6_40mm_Duct.stl 
OR 
E3D_v5_v6_40mm_Duct_Mirrored.stl

------------------------------------------------------

if you want the DUAL fan version print 
E3Dv5-Holder.stl OR E3Dv6-Holder.stl (pick your extruder version)
AND
Fan-Duck-Left - repaired - oriented.stl 
AND
Fan-Duck-Right - repaired - oriented.stl

This version of fan duct is not yet tested. 
Needs 4 screws M3 25mm long
2 fans 40mm left/right
1 fan 30 mm central
You might need also a zip tie in order to hold the hole system to the e3d barrel.

I am pretty sure that this version will work extremely well.
If you test it let me know in the comments below.

More info here : 
http://www.trustfm.net/hardware/3DPrinter.php
